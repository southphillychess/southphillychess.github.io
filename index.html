About Us

Welcome to the South Philly Chess Club! We are a growing community of chess enthusiasts, from complete beginners to seasoned players. Our club provides a fun, friendly and competitive environment to improve your skills, meet new people, and enjoy the intellectual challenge of chess. We organize regular meetups and tournaments.
